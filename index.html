Welcome to my corner of the webternets

Here at Strickly Research, I publish elegant facets of my life, research, and doodling for your pleasure.

Whether you arrived hear by a link I gave you or by some form of google stalking, feel free to poke around. If you want to find out more about me, I recommend digesting the website in the order of the menu. If you just want some technical drawings or Latex templates for inspiration, just follow the links.

As you have noticed, the thematic images are doodles of stringed instruments, especially violins. As a sometime-amateur sometimes-professional violinist and instructor, I am struck not only by the auditory pleasure but also the visual appeal, technical design, and operation of the instruments.
